1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when making a booking or inquiry, including name, email, phone, passport details (for permits), and health information relevant to trekking safety.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage your booking
  • To obtain necessary trekking permits
  • To communicate about your trip
  • To send relevant travel and safety updates
  • To improve our services

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with service partners necessary to fulfil your trek, and only to the extent required.

4. Data Retention

We retain booking records for 7 years for legal and tax compliance. Marketing data is held for 2 years unless you opt out earlier.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@summitpath.com. We respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for navigation and analytics cookies (with your consent). You may disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@summitpath.com or write to SummitPath, Thamel, Kathmandu, Nepal.
