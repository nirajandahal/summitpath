About SummitPath

Born in the mountains. Built on trust.

Our Story

SummitPath was founded in 2008 by a small team of Nepali mountaineers and international trekking enthusiasts who believed that the Himalayas deserved to be explored with deeper care — for the environment, the local communities, and the trekkers themselves.

What began as a two-guide operation running Everest Base Camp treks has grown into a full-service trekking company with over 40 routes across Nepal's most iconic regions. We've guided more than 3,400 trekkers from over 60 countries — from seasoned alpinists to first-time hikers.

Our Mission

To provide transformative mountain experiences that are safe, sustainable, and deeply connected to the culture and natural beauty of Nepal.

Our Values

  • Safety above all: Every decision on every trail is made with trekker safety as the first priority.
  • Authentic experience: We believe travel should create genuine connections, not just Instagram moments.
  • Environmental responsibility: We are committed to Leave No Trace principles and offset all group emissions.
  • Fair wages & community: Our guides earn above-market rates and we invest 5% of revenues into local schools and trail maintenance.

Certifications & Memberships

SummitPath holds full licensing from Nepal's Department of Tourism. Our senior guides are certified by the Nepal Mountaineering Association. We are proud members of TAAN and the Sustainable Tourism Alliance.
